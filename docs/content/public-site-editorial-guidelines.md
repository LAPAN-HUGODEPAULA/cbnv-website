# Public Site Editorial Guidelines

## Metadata

- Change ID: `produce-public-site-content-round-1`
- Scope: CBNV 2026 public website
- Language: Brazilian Portuguese
- Status: round-1 editorial guidance for implementation and consistency review

## Voice

The public site SHOULD sound scientific, institutional, sober, welcoming and precise.

Use direct sentences. Prefer concrete information about dates, venue, theme, submission phase and external links. Avoid promotional pressure and vague promises.

The copy SHOULD help visitors decide what to do next without implying that pending services are already available.

## Terms To Avoid

Avoid unsupported scale or superiority claims:

- maior evento de neurovisão da América Latina;
- principal evento da área;
- imperdível;
- revolucionário;
- transforme sua carreira;
- garanta sua vaga, while registration is not open;
- fronteira do conhecimento, unless the sentence names the specific research frontier;
- workshops, unless an official program record confirms workshops.

Avoid copy that implies the website performs services that are external to the platform:

- pagamento pelo site;
- certificado emitido pelo site;
- QR code gerado pelo site;
- hospedagem de vídeos completos no site.

## Confirmed Vs Pending Language

Use confirmed language only when the fact is present in canonical settings, program seed, or approved source documents.

Recommended labels:

| Status | Recommended copy |
|---|---|
| Confirmed | confirmado; informação confirmada |
| Public but preliminary | programação preliminar; sujeito a atualização |
| Missing public link | em breve |
| Operational channel unavailable | canal oficial a definir |
| Person/details not public | participantes a confirmar |
| Not in this site | processo externo; plataforma externa |

Do not fill unknown links with `#`. If a link is missing, use `em breve` or a disabled state.

## CTAs

Use CTAs that match the actual state.

Ready links:

- Ver programação
- Ver palestrantes
- Acessar inscrições
- Enviar mensagem
- Abrir no Google Maps
- Ver edições anteriores
- Ver anais
- Ver playlist

Pending links:

- Inscrições em breve
- Submissões em breve
- Link em breve
- Canal oficial a definir
- Detalhes em breve

Do not use high-pressure CTAs while dates, prices or capacity rules are not published.

## Accessibility And Readability

Copy SHOULD be scannable in narrow layouts:

- keep hero and card copy concise;
- use one idea per paragraph;
- avoid long subordinate clauses;
- write link text that describes the destination;
- avoid relying on color-only status language;
- use sentence case in body text and avoid all-caps outside short labels.

## Institutional Acknowledgements

Footer and support sections SHOULD mention institutional support without turning the footer into a second About page.

Recommended FAPEMIG acknowledgement:

> Apoio institucional: FAPEMIG - Fundacao de Amparo a Pesquisa do Estado de Minas Gerais.

If the process number is approved for public display, use:

> Apoio institucional: FAPEMIG - Fundacao de Amparo a Pesquisa do Estado de Minas Gerais. Processo OET-00394-26.

## Previous Editions

Previous editions MUST be framed as historical archive. Dates, locations, themes, playlists and proceedings from previous editions SHALL NOT be written in a way that can be mistaken for current CBNV 2026 information.

Use labels such as:

- Acervo historico
- Edicao anterior
- Materiais de edicoes anteriores
- Anais da edicao
- Playlist da edicao

## Content Quality Checklist

Before implementing a copy block, check:

- Is the statement true according to current sources?
- Is the fact necessary on this page?
- Is the wording specific enough?
- Does the page already say the same thing?
- Does it avoid inflated claims?
- Does it separate confirmed and pending information?
- Does it fit the current section without layout redesign?
- Does it identify the right target field or template location?
